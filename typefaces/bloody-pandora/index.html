About Bloody Pandora

This typeface is inspired by all the great sans serif fonts and typefaces I have used and loved ever since I first started using a computer back in 1988. It's not any particular kind of flavour of sans serif (geometric, humanist, etc.), but a plain straightforward work horse intended sans. Good for everything.

It's still a work in progress. Things to come are italics, another script, maybe some different widths, certainly a monospace version and whatever else I fancy adding. Bloody Pandora and her box. Which is how this typeface got its name.

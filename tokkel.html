About Tokkel

Tokkel is a reversed contrast sans-serif typeface. It started of with a nice looking o and it ended up looking like it was born in the 70s. Not a bad decade to be related to.

Tokkel is a display typeface that works best for short sections of text and headlines. It has several weights from thin to black. The width of the glyphs remains constant between all weights.
